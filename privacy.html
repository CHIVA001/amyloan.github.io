Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Amy Loan ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and the services provided through it.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it. By downloading, registering, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our loan services, verify your identity, and process your requests, we collect several types of information:

A. Personal Identification and KYC (Know Your Customer) Information

To comply with financial regulations and assess loan applications, we require:

  • Full Name: First Name and Last Name.
  • Demographic Details: Gender, Date of Birth, Marital Status, and Education Level.
  • Government ID: ID Type (e.g., National ID Card), ID Number, ID Expiry Date, and photographs of the front and back of your ID Card.
  • Verification Photos: A real-time selfie photo to verify that your identity matches your government ID card.

B. Contact Information

  • Personal Contact Details: Phone Number, Email Address, and Physical Address (Street Number, Province, Current Province, etc.).
  • Social Media Details: Optional links or identifiers to facilitate communications.

C. Occupational and Income Information

To assess repayment capacity and manage financial risk:

  • Employment Details: Working Type, Business Type, Working Status, and Years of Experience.
  • Financial Details: Monthly Income, Date of Income, and Method of Payment (Bank transfer or Cash).
  • Employer Contact Info: Company Name, Company Phone Number, Position, and Company Address.

D. Guarantor Information

Details of designated guarantors (e.g., Guarantor Type, Contact Info) provided by you to support your loan application. You must ensure you have the consent of any guarantor before providing their information.

E. Financial and Transactional Data

Details regarding your requested loan amount, term, repayment period, interest period, payment schedules, payment history, and wallet balance.

F. Device and Technical Information

  • Device Details: Device model, operating system, and OS version.
  • App Usage: Package information and version of the Amy Loan App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process and use your information for the following business and legal purposes:

  1. Identity Verification & KYC: To confirm your identity and prevent fraudulent registrations.
  2. Credit Assessment & Underwriting: To review your employment, income, and guarantor information to make decisions on loan approvals.
  3. Loan Management: To disburse approved loans, track payment schedules, calculate interest/fees, and manage late payments.
  4. Customer Support: To address issues, handle user questions, and connect you with our support team.
  5. Notifications: To send push notifications or local alerts regarding payment deadlines, application status updates, or account security.
  6. Regulatory Compliance: To comply with applicable financial laws, anti-money laundering (AML) regulations, and legal directives.

3. App Permissions & Data Access

To function correctly on your device and facilitate required document uploads, the App requests your explicit permission to access the following features:

Internet Access

Used to securely connect to our servers, fetch loan schedules, and transmit your application details.

Camera Access

Used to take a real-time selfie and capture photos of your ID Card (Front and Back) for identity verification.

Photo Library Access

Used to let you securely choose existing photos of supporting documents from your gallery and save your generated loan statements.

Push Notifications

Used to send critical alerts regarding upcoming payment deadlines, account status changes, and notifications from credit officers.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information under the following limited circumstances:

  • Internal Personnel: Authorized credit officers (COs) and administrators who evaluate and manage your loan request.
  • Credit Reporting Agencies & Regulatory Bodies: Where required by national financial regulations to report credit information or verify credit histories.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party partners who assist us in operations (e.g., hosting providers, SMS gateway providers), provided they are bound by strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal Compliance: When required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authorities to comply with legal processes or protect our rights and assets.

5. Data Security and Storage

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communication between the App and our backend servers is encrypted using Secure Sockets Layer/Transport Layer Security (SSL/TLS) protocols.
  • Secure Local Storage: Any data stored locally on your device (such as language preferences or session tokens) is managed using secure key-value stores.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel involved in the processing of loan applications have access to your personal files and documents.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal and financial information for as long as your account is active, or as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by applicable laws (which may require financial records to be retained for a specific minimum duration).

Account and Data Deletion Request: You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. To request deletion, you can contact us using the contact details provided below, or use the account deletion options if available in your app settings.

*Please Note: If you have an outstanding or active loan, we cannot delete your records until all financial obligations are fully settled. Furthermore, some data may be retained for legally mandated periods under national financial regulations.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights:

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw your consent to process certain categories of data (which may limit your ability to use the App's services).
